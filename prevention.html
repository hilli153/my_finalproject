Prevention

  • Vaccinations
    • Age Animal Vaccine Purpose
    6–8 weeks Dog Distemper, Parvovirus Protects against severe viral diseases affecting respiratory and digestive systems
    10–12 weeks Dog DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parvo, Parainfluenza) Boosts immunity against multiple core diseases
    12–16 weeks, followed by 1 year booster Dog Rabies Prevents rabies, a fatal zoonotic disease
    6–8 weeks Cat FVRCP (Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, Panleukopenia) Protects against common respiratory and gastrointestinal diseases
    10-12 weeks, followed by 1 year booster Cat Rabies Prevents rabies transmission to humans and animals
    Annual / 3-year Dog & Cat Booster Vaccines Maintains immunity over time
